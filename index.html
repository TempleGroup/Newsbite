HEALTH ON MY MIND
CHEESY
Healthy cheese may sound like an oxymoron, but it has some health benefits that might surprise you. Cheese is filled with healthy nutrients, many of which you may not get enough of, such as calcium, protein and vitamin B-12. But this isn’t a license to go crazy. The Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics suggests keeping a serving of cheese to 1.5 ounces, which is the size of four dice. Most people don’t need a reason to eat more cheese, but if you keep your serving sizes in check and choose a quality cheese, your health may benefit.
